Chanterelle

gallery

Chanterelle offers a unique contemporary setting to cater for birthday celebrations, corporate events, dinners, product launches and holiday parties for up to 50 people.

When it comes to drinks, our restaurant stores one of the largest selection of artisanal orange, rose, white and red wines from local vineyards. Bubbly prosecco is also on the menu, for special occasions.

Chanterelle is not your average french restaurant, partly because we serve some of the best gourmet dishes in town, and partly because our menu has a distinct flair.
